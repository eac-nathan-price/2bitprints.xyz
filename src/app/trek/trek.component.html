About LCARS:

LCARS (Library Computer Access/Retrieval System) is the computer system interface used throughout Starfleet in the 24th century. This interface recreates the distinctive orange and blue color scheme and typography used in Star Trek: The Next Generation and subsequent series.

Font Categories:

  • Federation: Official Starfleet and Federation fonts
  • TNG: The Next Generation series fonts
  • DS9: Deep Space Nine series fonts
  • TOS: The Original Series fonts
  • Movies: Star Trek movie fonts
  • Alien Races: Fonts representing different species
  • Technical: Engineering and technical display fonts
  • Additional: Other Star Trek themed fonts
